Mission Statement

Refining Spaces, an Interior Design firm, provides consultancy and furniture sourcing services for discerning residential and commercial clients. Furniture is sourced from reputed manufacturers overseas, with measures taken to ensure enduring quality and ergonomic designs that are aesthetic as well as functional.

Services Provided:
  • Consultation

  • Space planning

  • Furniture and furnishing selection

  • Drapery and window coverings

  • Wall Treatments

  • Flooring

  • Accessorizing


About The Designer


As a specialist in space planning and creative design ideas, Sangeeta focuses on offering cost-effective solutions and ideas for every space. Based on the current layout, design, and parameters of your home, she will suggest changes to give your home a novel and inviting appearance. On the other hand, if you already have a strong sense of what your design needs are, but are unsure of how to affect those changes in your home, she will work within your constraints—whether they are space-related, cost, or disability restrictions—as well as with your own unique requirements, style-needs, and aesthetic sensibilities to revitalize your home and turn it into an embodiment of the theme of your dreams.
